Royal Birkdale Golf Club, located in Southport, England, is one of the most prestigious courses in the world. Known for its challenging layout and rich history, it has hosted numerous major championships, including The Open Championship. The course record is a topic of great interest among golf enthusiasts, as it highlights the pinnacle of performance on this legendary links.
