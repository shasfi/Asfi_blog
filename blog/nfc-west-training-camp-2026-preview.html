NFC West Training Camp 2026: What to Watch For

The NFC West remains one of the most competitive divisions in the NFL, and the 2026 training camp season is no exception. With the San Francisco 49ers, Arizona Cardinals, Los Angeles Rams, and Seattle Seahawks all gearing up for the upcoming season, there are plenty of storylines to follow. Here’s a breakdown of the key narratives shaping each team’s camp.

San Francisco 49ers: Quarterback Competition and Defensive Adjustments

The 49ers enter training camp with questions at quarterback. Whether they’re evaluating a veteran or a young prospect, the battle under center will be a focal point. Additionally, the defense, known for its ferocious pass rush, may see adjustments to counter evolving offensive schemes across the league.

Offensive Line Health

Injuries have plagued the 49ers' offensive line in recent years. Keeping the unit healthy will be critical to establishing consistency in both the running and passing games.

Arizona Cardinals: Youth Movement and Scheme Changes

The Cardinals are in the midst of a youth movement, with several rookies and second-year players expected to take on larger roles. How quickly these young talents adapt to the NFL will determine the team’s trajectory. A new offensive or defensive scheme could also be in play, adding another layer of intrigue.

Wide Receiver Depth

With the potential departure or arrival of key receivers, the Cardinals’ passing game could look significantly different. Training camp will reveal how the new pieces fit together.

Los Angeles Rams: Veteran Leadership and Roster Turnover

The Rams have relied on veteran leadership to guide them through recent seasons, but roster turnover could test their depth. Key departures on defense and offense mean younger players will need to step up quickly.

Defensive Rebuild

The Rams’ defense, once a cornerstone of their success, may undergo a rebuild. Training camp will showcase whether new additions can fill the voids left by departing stars.

Seattle Seahawks: Coaching Adjustments and Offensive Identity

The Seahawks are under new leadership, with potential changes to their offensive philosophy. How the team adapts to these adjustments will be a major storyline. The running game, in particular, could see a resurgence with a renewed focus.

Secondary Questions

Seattle’s secondary, once known as the "Legion of Boom," has seen its share of changes. Training camp will reveal whether the current group can return to elite status.

Conclusion: NFC West Training Camp 2026 Outlook

The NFC West training camps for 2026 will be defined by quarterback battles, defensive adjustments, and roster turnover. Each team faces unique challenges, but the division’s competitive nature ensures plenty of drama as the season approaches.
