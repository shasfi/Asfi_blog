Sheikh Asfi

Founder & Editor, Asfi Blog

SA

Sheikh Asfi (Asfand Yar Ali) is the founder and editor of Asfi Blog, an independent news and technology publication covering trending topics in artificial intelligence, programming, web development, business, science, and health.

With a background in full-stack software engineering and a passion for breaking down complex stories into plain English, Asfi writes for readers who want to understand what's happening in technology and the world — without the jargon or hype.

At Asfi Blog, every story is original, fact-checked, and optimized for both human readers and AI-powered answer engines. The publication covers breaking news, trending topics, and evergreen guides that help students, developers, and professionals stay informed about the latest in AI, web development, and global trends.

Background

Asfi is a final-year software engineering student at UMT (University of Management and Technology), Gujranwala, Pakistan, specializing in Laravel/PHP, React, and AI-integrated full-stack web development. He's completed internships at Elevvo, Apexify Technologies, and MSN Academy, and is actively building a freelance and agency portfolio.

Asfi Blog itself is one of his projects — built end-to-end, from the trend-detection and auto-publishing pipeline to the front-end you're reading right now.
